About The Festival

Some interesting facts about the music festival

  • The festival is held on the world famous German race track Nurburgring.
  • There are three stages on the territory of Rock am Ring. Headliners perform on the main stage, famous rockers perform on the middle stage, and little — known musicians perform on the small stage.
  • The festival was first held in 1985. Since then, the popularity of the festival has only grown.
  • There is a tradition of shouting the name Helga at Rock am Ring. A few years ago, a customer who lost a friend named Helga in the crowd ran around the neighborhood all night shouting her name.
